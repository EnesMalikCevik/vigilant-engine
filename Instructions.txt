Goal of this game is to gain 20 points as soon as possible. 

You start with 3 health and in case of losing all 3 of them, the game ends.


Balls by Colour:


Green: +1 point

Pink: +2 points

Gold: -1 point (Yeah, gold isn't worthy always)

Red: -1 health

Black: Either increases your player's speed or decreases it




Gameplay:

Right-Left arrow buttons or A-D buttons to move your character.
